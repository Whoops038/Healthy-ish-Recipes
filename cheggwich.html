Vegan Scrambled Egg

   
Time

Total time: 12-17 min

Prep time: 7-10 min (depends how good you are at chopping stuff)

cook time: 2-3 min

Clean + pack-away time: 3-4 min without dishwasher

   
Recipe

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Seperately boil water(wherever) & 1/4 cup milk in pot
  2. Let 1/2 chicken breast dry.
  3. Beat 2 eggs and stir
  4. Bring milk to simmer and pour in eggs. Stir gently. Once the mixture is not liquid, turn off the fire and set it aside
  5. Toast 2 slices bread.
  6. Put 2 pcs lettuce on one piece of toast, add egg, then 2 pc lettuce again
  7. Put chicken in pot, pour 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar over chicken, add the boiling water and boil on medium heat
  8. Add 1 tsp of kala namak to get that egg taste. Optionally add 1-3 dashes of turmeric to get that egg color. Stir.
  9. Add 1 tbsp vinegar to the boiling water, put in chicken, then boil on medium heat
  10. Press sandwich down if necessary and enjoy.

Changing recipe suggestions:

  • only 1 tbsp of milk is required per egg. Less milk = cooks faster
  • Any milk goes. Same volume. Water works too, same volume
  • Add whatever herbs and spices
  • Add whatever other toppings to the sandwich. They should small and/or thin, though/li>
