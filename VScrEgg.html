Banana Blondie

   
Time

Total time: 12-17 min

Prep time: 7-10 min (depends how good you are at chopping stuff)

cook time: 2-3 min

Clean + pack-away time: 3-4 min without dishwasher

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. 1. Chop garlic and some of the onion. There should be around twice as much garlic as onion
  2. 2. Tear the rest of the onion into pieces
  3. 3. Crumble the tofu, and add the milk. It is better to have chunks too big since you can further crush them in the pan, than to have chunks too small.
  4. 4. Heat a pan on medium heat and add the oil. Rotate the pan to help the oil spread all over
  5. 7. Add the chopped garlic and onion and stir
  6. 8. Add the tomato flesh and stir
  7. 9. Add the tofu-milk mixture and stir. Crumble tofu further if necessary
  8. 10. Add 1 tsp of kala namak to get that egg taste. Optionally add 1-3 dashes of turmeric to get that egg color. Stir.
  9. 11. Add the sliced tomato and onion and stir.
  10. 12. When the sliced tomato & onion have halved in size, start cooking off heat.

Changing recipe suggestions:

  • Add a bit of citrus (e.g. vinegar/lemon/lime)
  • Add herbs & spices of your choice
  • Add whatever other toppings you want. Note: more toppings = more oil needed
  • Use butter or margarine instead of oil, same volume
