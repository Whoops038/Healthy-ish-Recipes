Cauliflower Rice Bowl

   
Time

Total time: 25-40 min, depending if others are cooking as well

Prep time: 7-20 min (depends how good you are at chopping stuff)

cook time: 13 min

Clean + pack-away time: 5-7 min without dishwasher

   
Recipe

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Boil water
  2. Cut chicken into strips and marinate in baking soda, salt & rice vinegar. Add a little water too
  3. Chop garlic and slice onion & mushrooms
  4. Boil cauliflower to soften, then chop and crush/blend.
  5. Simmer water and poach chicken in it
  6. Use a little water to pan-"steam" cauliflower rice. Transfer to bowl
  7. Cut chicken to check for tenderness, stop cooking when tender.
  8. Heat garlic and onion in pan, using water. Add a bit more water to sautee the other veggies.
  9. Add chicken & veg to the rice
  10. Crack egg in bowl. If you are talented enough, skip this step
  11. Heat sesame oil in pan, then pour in the egg, placing the yolk in the centre. Or crack egg over pan if you can do it well.

Changing recipe suggestions:

  • Change amount of or remove garlic
  • Marinating chicken not necessary, is to keep tenderness (baking soda) and prevent too much bursting during boiling (vinegear, salt)
  • Can swap oil for same volume of other type of fat, or use a non-stick pan
  • Can fry in oil/butter rather than "steam" in water. 1-2tsp fat
  • Add whatever vegetables. Note some veggies take longer to cook so fat may be needed and pan-"steaming" won't work (unless using non-stick pan)
  • Using lower cholesterol eggs
  • Chilli flakes would be great with this
